# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
## AI-Native Cloud Architecture

## Overview

My ideal customers are SaaS companies building or scaling AI-powered products that rely heavily on Cloud infrastructure and need architectural clarity to ensure reliability, scalability, and cost efficiency.

These organizations operate in fast-moving environments where architectural decisions directly impact product velocity, system reliability, and Cloud cost.

They understand that architecture is a strategic asset, not just an implementation detail.

---

## Company Characteristics

### Industry

— SaaS (B2B or developer-focused SaaS)  
— AI-native startups and scaleups  
— developer platforms and infrastructure companies  
— data platforms and AI tooling companies  

### Company Stage

— Seed to Series C startups  
— profitable SaaS companies scaling their platform  
— engineering teams actively expanding infrastructure  

### Company Size

— 5 to 100 engineers  
— 10 to 500 total employees  

These companies are large enough to have real architectural complexity, but small enough to move fast and make decisions quickly.

---

## Technical Characteristics

These companies typically:

— run production workloads on AWS, Azure, or GCP  
— are building or integrating AI-powered features  
— operate containerized or Cloud-native infrastructure  
— have growing Cloud cost and infrastructure complexity  
— need scalable, reliable, and maintainable system architecture  

They often face challenges such as:

— unclear or evolving architecture  
— increasing Cloud cost  
— scaling bottlenecks  
— reliability or performance issues  
— lack of clear architectural direction

## Business Triggers (When they need my help)

Ideal customers typically experience one or more of the following situations:

— scaling engineering teams rapidly  
— introducing AI capabilities into their platform  
— experiencing rising Cloud cost  
— encountering performance or scalability issues  
— needing architectural clarity before scaling further  
— planning major infrastructure or platform changes  

These moments create urgency and strong demand for architectural expertise.
